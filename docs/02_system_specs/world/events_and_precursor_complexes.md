# Events and Precursor Complexes

## Purpose

World events and precursor complexes provide punctuated mystery, temporary pressure and access to exceptional rewards.

## Events

### Design philosophy

Events should always combine:
- threat
- opportunity
- atmosphere
- lore resonance

### Example event families

- Precursor echoes: rare temporary signal windows with valuable loot or data
- spore synchronization: beautiful but dangerous nights with altered toxicity and aggression
- dead Goliath passage: terrain-changing macro event that creates new opportunities
- magnetic storms, migrations, meteor showers, spore surges

## Precursor complexes

### Role

Precursor complexes are exploration-dungeon spaces that:
- escalate mystery
- provide lore and rare technology
- gate extraordinary rewards

### Core loop

1. discover entrance
2. enter dark, inert complex
3. restore or connect power
4. awaken systems and risks
5. choose between partial loot and full exploration

## Acceptance criteria

- events feel authored by the world rather than random timers
- complexes are materially different from surface play
- rewards justify risk and reinforce progression

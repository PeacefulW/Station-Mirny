# Audio Direction

## Core philosophy

Audio is one of the primary carriers of atmosphere and safety contrast.

## Exterior sound identity

- persistent wind
- distant creature calls
- hostile emptiness
- pressure from breath and equipment

## Interior sound identity

- generators
- relays
- air systems
- the mechanical hum of temporary safety

## Special spaces

### Precursor complexes

Should emphasize:
- oppressive silence
- mechanical awakening
- dread through sparse sound design

### Major events

Events should have immediately recognizable sonic identity, not just visual changes.

## Music

Music should stay restrained and atmospheric, rising mainly to support danger, revelation or homecoming.

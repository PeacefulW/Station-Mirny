# Fauna Catalog

## Core framing

Planetary fauna are feral descendants of once-functional bioengineering tools.

## Main entries

### Cleaners

- aggressive
- blind / hearing-focused
- sanitation origin
- currently interpret human machinery and noise as contamination

### Filterers

- peaceful megafauna
- atmospheric cleaning role
- associated with cleaner air and possible adaptation-path transport

### Burrowers

- underground or foundation-threatening fauna family
- future infrastructure pressure role

### Glow fauna

- small peaceful luminescent species
- environmental readability role

### Goliaths

- colossal biomechanical landscape entities
- awe, scale and terrain disruption role

## Content rules

- fauna should feel alien but purposeful
- visual identity should imply ecological function
- each major species should teach something about the world
